6.5. Birth and Death Processes (cont'd)

As we see, there are two main types of birth and death processes: queueing system and population model. THe key difference between them is that the birth rate in the queueing system is typically a constant (does not depend on the current state iii), while the birth rate in population model increases as iii increases.
